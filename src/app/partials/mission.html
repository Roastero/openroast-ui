Our Mission

Currently, there is a sizeable barrier of entry for home coffee roasting. Openroast's mission is to make home coffee roasting accessible to everyone, not just the brave at heart. In order to make this mission a reality, Openroast seeks to create a platform where users can create and share roast recipes that interface with our software and supported roasters. In this way, a novice roaster can download a recipe with great reviews, purchase the green coffee the recipe calls for, and then achieve the same quality an experienced roaster achieves. Additionally, Openroast looks to provide ample resources to facilitate users to move from using other usersâ€™ recipes to creating their own, thus eliminating the barrier of entry to home coffee roasting.
